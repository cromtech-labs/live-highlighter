How to use

  • Add a group: Click "Add Group" to create a new highlight group
  • Add words: Click a group to expand it, then add words to highlight
  • Priority: Drag groups to reorder - higher groups have priority when text overlaps
  • Edit: Click on group name or color to edit - changes save automatically
  • Toggle: Use the switch on each group to enable/disable it
  • Delete: Click the × button to remove a group or word
